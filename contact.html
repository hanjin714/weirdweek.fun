Get in Touch

Contact

We respond to editorial corrections, rights concerns, and genuine feedback.

Email

For corrections, policy questions, rights concerns, or feedback about our editorial content, contact us at:

hello@weirdweek.fun

What to Include

To help us respond quickly, please include:

  • The specific page URL or issue number you are referencing
  • A clear description of your concern or request
  • For rights-related requests: the specific image or content in question and your relationship to it

Response Time

We aim to respond within 3-5 business days. Rights-related requests are prioritized. Spam, solicitation, and promotional emails will not receive a response.

Social Presence

WeirdWeek does not currently maintain active social media accounts. All official communication is through the email above. Accounts on third-party platforms claiming to represent WeirdWeek should be verified before engaging.

Product Submissions

If you have found an unusual product you think belongs in a future issue, you are welcome to email us the listing URL. We do not guarantee inclusion, and we do not accept payment or incentives for coverage.
