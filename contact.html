Contact WeirdWeek

We respond to corrections, source suggestions, rights concerns, and genuine editorial feedback.

Email

Send messages to hello@weirdweek.fun. Include the page URL and a clear description of your correction or request.

Corrections

If a factual claim needs review, send the claim, the page URL, and any supporting source. We prioritize corrections over general pitches.

Rights concerns

If you believe content on the site creates a rights issue, include the URL, the specific material, and your relationship to it.

Topic suggestions

You may suggest strange systems, old technology, infrastructure stories, or design questions. We do not accept paid placement.
